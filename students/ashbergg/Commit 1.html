    In Massachusetts, there are two types of orders you may be eligible to receive if you feel that you are experiencing some sort of imminent harm or abuse.  A 209A Abuse Prevention order may be awarded if you are in a situation with the person causing abuse where you are married, have children, are related or are living together.  A 258E Harassment Prevention order is awarded in different situations where you and the person committing the harassment are neighbors or friends. Think about the relationship you have with he person hurting you to figure out which order best suits your situation.
